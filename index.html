About Marshall

A Fighter for
North Florida

Marshall Rawson is a proud North Florida native, a husband, father of five, and a fearless conservative fighter. A graduate of the University of Florida's Levin College of Law, Marshall has dedicated his career and life to defending the values that make Florida great.

As president of the Young Americans for Liberty chapter in college, Marshall championed Second Amendment rights, limited government, and constitutional principles long before entering the political arena. He brings the same tenacity to his campaign for the Florida State House.

Marshall is the only candidate in this race who has physically defended our nation at the Texas-Mexico border, standing shoulder to shoulder with those protecting American sovereignty. He has fought to preserve Florida's heritage — defending historic veterans' monuments in Lake City and Gainesville, and the Ten Commandments monument in Starke.

A committed community leader, Marshall has worked directly with local officials to remove inappropriate materials from county libraries and stands firmly against the radical ideologies threatening our children's education. His roots run deep in North Florida, and he's ready to take that fight to Tallahassee.

My Mission

Why I'm Running

I am running because every day, the Florida that I love loses another 800-1000 acres of good greenspace and agriculture lands to soulless suburban development.
I will be Florida's greatest advocate when it comes to conservation and Florida Agriculture. Shopping malls and soulless suburban development are replacing the land we love — and I will fight to stop it.
I am running because government wants you to pay for unchecked growth by increasing your taxes and further regulating your business.
While government makes room for the whole world to relocate here, that same government wants you to pay for it — inevitably increasing crime and congestion. I will be your Florida First Candidate.
I am running because local government is using your tax dollars to push anti-Western and anti-Christian propaganda on our youth.
I am sick of seeing local government take the fruits of our labor, by taxing us, so that they can fund county libraries and education curriculums that push sick, woke propaganda on our children. I have worked with my county commissioner to remove such propaganda from the children's tables at our county library and I will continue that fight.
I am running because the most innocent and vulnerable of our society desperately need equal protection in Tallahassee.
I will staunchly defend life as I did against last year's proposed Amendment 4. The unborn deserve a voice, and I will be that voice.
I am running because Tallahassee has repeatedly failed to defend and restore our Second Amendment rights.
I will immediately work to repeal the prohibition on 18-20 year olds from buying a firearm as well as the prohibition on where and in what manner a law-abiding adult carries. As the Young Americans for Liberty president in college I championed these issues, and I will in Tallahassee.
I am running because Tallahassee needs the courage to defend our historic veterans' monuments and the Ten Commandments.
I have defended these good things all across Florida and elsewhere — including our heroic historic veterans' monuments in Lake City, Gainesville and our Ten Commandments in Starke. The radical left will not erase our heritage on my watch.
I am the only one in this race who has defended our country at the Texas/Mexico border.
On Day 1 I am ready to enact policy and support our Governor and U.S. President in ensuring that we have the most safe and secure border on planet earth!
— Marshall
